What We Do

Paani is an NGO focused on bringing comprehensive sanitary relief to Pakistan

We are working to improve the sanitary crisis in Pakistan by providing sources of clean energy and clean water. We do this by building wells, installing solar panels, designing curriculum for youth, and providing healthcare technologies that are both eco-friendly and impactful.

In order to achieve our goals, Paani relies on our membership base to design curriculum, increase outreach, secure funding, and arrange events.

What We Are Up To...

  • Water - As the water crisis has lead 84% of the population lacking access to safe drinkable water, Paani is working to improve this situation by building wells.
  • Solar - As Pakistan faces an electric crisis that has affected millions, Paani is working to improve that by installing solar panels.
  • Education - Paani has formed a partnership to create and deliver curriculum regarding the importance of applying the principles of public health and sanitation
  • Health - Paani has developed relationships with four rural health clinics across Pakistan that provide low to no-cost treatment for patients. Paani supplements these clinics with health needs ranging from sanitation supplies to innovative health technologies.
  • Waste - Due to issues with policy and education, Pakistan deals with severe waste concerns. In an effort to relieve these, Paani is working to instill programs in Pakistan targeted at removing waste while empowering Pakistani citizens.
